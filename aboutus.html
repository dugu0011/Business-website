About Us

in permanent damage, if proper care is not taken. With decades of experience in the cleaning industry, our team of experts at Cobbler & Co.
are more than capable to handle all your designer pieces with utmost care and precision, and restore them back to life. Our services include designer
shoe & bag cleaning; delicate leather, suede & fur cleaning and restoration. Providing unmatched quality at extremely reasonable prices makes us your ideal
choice for all kind of restoration jobs. Be it your daily routine wear or exquisite designer collection, at Cobbler & Co. we strive very hard to meet all your expectations
of shoe & bag care.
